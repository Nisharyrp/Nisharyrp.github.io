Welcome to BECS

Welcome to Back ward children educational socity (BECS). Founded in the year 2018 by Nishar Basha D, a software engineer who has the intension to help poor chilren/students by providing the basic needs.

Backward Children Education Society

In a modernizing world education is the only means to secure the future. Nishar Basha who is a software engineer, who has seen struggles of his parents Mastan Vali D and Ameena Bee D for his enducation. Keeping this in mind, Nishar started with Backward children educational society along with his childhood friends Siva kumar, Shankar Reddy, Harish and Siva from a tiny village name called Yerra Palli which is in Chittor district, Andra Pradesh. Nishar and his friends whated to broaden it as much as possible and provide their help and support to the students who are in need.

BECS team started providing evening tutions to the studens in yerappalli village before even BECS official registation. BECS was officially registed in Chittor district collector office in the year 2018. The Chairman of BECS is Nishar Basha and vice president is Shankar Reddy.
